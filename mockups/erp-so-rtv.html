Return to vendor

Ship stock out of the warehouse back to a supplier or to the owning client — recalls, defects, expired or over-delivered goods — distinct from a customer outbound (which ships to a consignee). Each line picks a stock plate; shipping issues the quantity out of stock, logs the audit trail and produces an RTV note.
