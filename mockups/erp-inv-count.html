Count Approval

Cycle-count sheets counted on the floor (PWA) or keyed here — one sheet can span many bins. The system computes the variance between counted and system quantity per plate; a supervisor approves to correct stock to the counted figure, or rejects to leave it unchanged and trigger a recount. Approvals post to the audit trail.

Filters
Sheets whose largest plate variance is within tolerance can be bulk-approved; larger variances are flagged ⚠ review and must be opened individually.
