Disposal / Scrap

First-class write-off of blocked or expired stock — scrap, destroy or write-off — replacing the generic quantity-decrease in Adjustments. Each disposal is reason-coded, requires supervisor approval by a different user (maker-checker), and on approval removes the quantity from the plate and writes the audit trail.
