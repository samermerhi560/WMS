Allocation

Reserve stock against each outbound order. The system allocates by FEFO (earliest expiry first) for expiry-tracked products and falls back to FIFO (oldest plate first) when a product carries no expiry. The recommended allocation is pre-filled; override any line manually. Confirm reserves the stock and flips the order to allocated, ready to pick. (Or do it all in one pass on Express Fulfil.)
