Returns / Put-back

Stock re-entering inventory: a put-back of over-picked or unused stock, or a customer return after dispatch. Each line is re-inspected and given a disposition — restock (back to available) or block as quarantine/damaged. Processing creates the plate(s) and writes the audit trail.
