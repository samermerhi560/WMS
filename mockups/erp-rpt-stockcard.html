Stock Card / Product Ledger

Complete audit ledger for a single product (and optionally one lot) — every movement in chronological order: receive, putaway, move, transfer, status change (quarantine / hold / damaged / expired), adjustment, count, repack, return and dispatch — each with a signed quantity and the running on-hand balance. Reads the DB.txns[] audit log; the balance is anchored to live on-hand.
