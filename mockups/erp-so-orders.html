Outbound Orders

Client requests to remove stock — a full stock-out or a partial, line-item exit. Flow: create the order, then allocate (FEFO for expiry-tracked stock, FIFO otherwise), pick / dispatch, and issue a delivery note. All actions have a full ERP manual fallback. Statuses: open → allocated → picking → picked → dispatched.
