Adjustments & Corrections

Correct stock that was received or handled wrong. A quantity adjustment changes how much is on a plate (damage, loss, found stock); an attribute correction fixes what it is — a wrong lot, expiry, serial, product or owning client keyed at receipt. Both are reason-coded, require supervisor approval, and are written to the audit trail.
