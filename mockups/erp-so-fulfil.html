Express Fulfil

The one-pass outbound flow: the system auto-allocates by FEFO/FIFO, you confirm picks (and serials where required), and a single Confirm fulfilment reserves, picks and dispatches in one step. The classic Allocation → Pick / Dispatch path stays available for separated teams — both are offered per order. Serial-tracked lines and short/blocked stock always pause for review. (Access to this one-pass flow will be role-gated in the build phase.)
