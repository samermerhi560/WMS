Putaway

One task per license plate sitting in the wait/put location with status to putaway (the good stock from receiving), for the operator's site. Select an LPN — the system suggests locations home → consolidate → category, filtered by capacity and segregation, then confirm flips the stock to available. Mixed pallets (one license plate, many products) are listed separately and decompose in any order you choose — tap the product you want to place next; the system mints one child LPN per line. Completed keeps the putaway history (what went where, by whom) with label reprint. Manual entry is the full ERP fallback when scanners are offline.
