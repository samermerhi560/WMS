Pick / Dispatch

Pick the allocated stock and confirm dispatch. On the floor this runs on the PWA (scan location → product → qty → serials); here is the full ERP manual fallback. Record picked quantities (and serials for serial-tracked products), then confirm dispatch — the stock is issued (removed from inventory) and a delivery note is produced. Short picks are captured as discrepancies. (Allocation + pick + dispatch can also be done in one pass on Express Fulfil.)
